Illinois Tech Esports

History

Originally formed in 2013, Illinois Tech Esports is an established, non-profit collegiate esports organization that has quickly grown to be seen as a regional leader known for its community focus and high-quality events.

Mission

To engage the community in casual and competitive gaming - we strive to create experiences dedicated to the player and aim to provide value for both gamers and our sponsors. Our goal is to create a space for everyone to connect through gaming.

Events

Every semester, our club hosts a variety of events, such as:

  • LANs
  • Watch parties
  • Tournaments
  • Retreats

and more!
